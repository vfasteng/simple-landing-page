  • Try it out with our interactive demo version which you can access with just a single click. Connect solid 3D models with a few clicks and inspect their relative movement. In the full version, the connected models will be your own and will be available for download immediately.

  • When two CAD Models are connected by a set of physics constraints, their relative motion is defined. But what we have done is create these connections between reference planes onto which the moving parts are mounted.

  • Users can easily create aniimations of their parts moving in an animation, or reacting to an impulse from an external source. Programmed animations can be used to drive other parts,

  • Switch to a Physics-based environment with a single button click to see how the assembly reacts to Gravity. Change and adjust the mechanical properties of the parts in the assembly to develop certain characteristics of the mechanism. Easily move anchor points without re-designing geometry.

  • Exporting your work could not be easier. Export a single joint or the entire scene to a single GLTF file with a single click of a button. Then just place it into a Physics scene and watch it come to life.

  • The App creates 3D scenes in a format compatible with WebVR and the SteamVR platforms. It is WebGL based and can run in any browser or device. It automatically senses which controllers are being used and activates the appropriate drivers.

This service compliments any 3D System and will work with yours.

It also works as a standalone product, and is all you need to create, build and develop an assembly of moving 3D Models.
